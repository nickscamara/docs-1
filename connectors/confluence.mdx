The `connector_id` for Confluence is `confluence`.

## Authorization Type
Psychic uses [OAuth 2.0](https://developer.atlassian.com/server/confluence/confluence-oauth2-provider-api/) to connect to Confluence.

## Configuration

### Custom OAuth Credentials
By default, Psychic will use our Confluence OAuth credentials to connect to your customer's accounts. If you want to use your own credentials instead, you can contact us at support@psychic.dev


## Synchronization
Confluence Documents can be fetched as-neded from the `/get-documents` endpoint. 
It's also possible to set up 24 hour syncs by providing a webhook URL in the [dashboard](https://dashboard.psychic.dev/syncs)
